Your Solar Appliance is Ready to Ship!

Your house's electrical system can accommodate a PV Appliance of up to {{ max_electrical_system_capacity_kW}} kW in size, and your roof can accommodate a PV appliance of up to {{ max_roof_capacity}} kW in size. Based on your electricity use, you could use a system of up to {{ desired_system_capacity}} kW. Based on these constraints, we recommend an appliance of {{ system_capacity}} kW in size, with a {{ smartbox_size}} A SmartBox. This will provide {{ system_production }} kWh per year, or {{ percent_of_electric_bill}} % of your annual electricity use.



Here's what we'll ship to you:

AC PV Modules

Your PV appliance will have {{number_of_modules}} modules of 240 watts each, for a total capacity of {{system_capacity}} kW. These modules will snap together using touch-safe cabling. Touch-safe, weatherproof cabling will connect the complete array to SafeConnect's proprietary SmartBox, which will ensure that a safe number of modules are installed and continuosly monitor your system for any electrical danger.

SmartBox and Cable

The "brains" of you PV appliance will be SafeConnect's proprietary safety smartbox, which will ensure the system is installed correctly before allowing it to operate. We'll also ship the touch-safe, plug-and-play cables needed to connect the AC PV modules to each other and a {{cable_length}}-foot cable to connect the modules to the SmartBox.

Roof Mounting

Your PV appliance will attach to your {{roof_type}} roof using {{number_of_modules}} footers, which will be bolted to roof rafters and flashed. We'll help you schedule a local roofer to install the footers for you after you review your financing options.

PV Plug

Like all SafeConnect Solar appliances, your appliance will be connected to 240 V "PV Plug" wired by an electrician to your load center. We'll help you schedule an electrician to get this done after you review your financing options.
